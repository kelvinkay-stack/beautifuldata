World's Tallest Buildings

The architectural height of the world's tallest completed buildings, from the first steel-frame giants of New York to today's Gulf and Asian supertalls. New York owned this board for seven decades before the crown moved to Chicago, Kuala Lumpur, Taipei and finally Dubai, where the Burj Khalifa still towers 149 m over its nearest rival.
