Science & Infrastructure/1883–2025

Longest Suspension Bridge Spans

The main spans of the world's longest suspension bridges, from Brooklyn's pioneering steel-wire crossing to the two-kilometre giants of today. America owned this board for nearly a century — the George Washington doubled the record in one leap — before the crown moved to Japan's Akashi Kaikyō and finally to Turkey's Çanakkale 1915, the first span past 2,000 m.

Source: Structurae / published span records Figures are main-span lengths between tower centres, not total bridge length. Updated 2026-07-09
