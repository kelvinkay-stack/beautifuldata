Most-Visited Countries

International tourist arrivals since 1980: France grabs No. 1 around 1990 and never lets go, while the rest of the board churns — Hungary and Canada fade from 1980s glory, China and Turkey storm up in the 2000s, and Japan's late boom sets a record in 2024. In between, COVID-19 vaporizes roughly 70% of all travel in a single year.

Source: UN World Tourism Organization (UN Tourism) Overnight tourist arrivals. Some 2020–2024 figures (notably France and China) are official estimates; Hungary's 1980s–90s counts reflect older methodology. Updated 2026-07-07
